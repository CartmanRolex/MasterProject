\section{Methodology}
\label{sec:methodology}

\subsection{Robot and Simulation Setup}

We use the \textbf{SO-101} arm simulated in \textbf{NVIDIA Isaac Sim}. The task is defined as picking a randomly placed orange from a table and placing it on a plate.

\subsection{Dataset Collection}

Demonstrations are recorded with \texttt{dataset\_recorder.py}. Each episode stores RGB observations from two cameras and joint-position actions at \SI{30}{\hertz}.

\subsection{Dataset Editing}

A custom GUI (\texttt{dataset-editor/}) allows manual episode inspection, trimming, and re-labelling. Episodes with failures or annotation errors are removed before training.
